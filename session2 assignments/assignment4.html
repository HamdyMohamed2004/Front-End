RTL vs. RFS

Feature RTL (Right-to-Left) RFS (Responsive Font Size)
Definition RTL layout is used for languages that are written from right to left, such as Arabic, Hebrew, Persian, and Urdu. RFS is a tool in Bootstrap that automatically resizes text based on the screen size.
Benefits Enhances readability and user experience for users of RTL languages, making the web content accessible and user-friendly for a broader audience. Ensures that the text remains readable and visually appealing across different devices, improving the overall user experience.
